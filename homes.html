HOMES Program Overview

Home Efficiency Upgrades

The HOMES program supports comprehensive home efficiency improvements including insulation, air sealing, and heating and cooling system upgrades. Rebates are based on actual energy savings achieved through your project.

HOMES Program

Home Efficiency Upgrades

Up to $16,000

The HOMES program provides rebates for whole-home efficiency improvements that achieve at least 20% modeled energy savings. The program rewards deeper energy savings with higher rebate amounts.

Maximum Rebate Per household
$16,000
Minimum Savings Required
20%
Rebate Structure Performance-based
$2,000-$4,000

Eligible Improvements

HOMES program covers efficiency upgrades that reduce whole-home energy consumption.

Insulation upgrades

Attic, wall, and floor insulation to reduce energy loss.

Air sealing

Eliminate drafts and improve building envelope performance.

HVAC efficiency

High-efficiency heating and cooling system upgrades.

Windows and doors

Energy-efficient windows and door replacements.

How Rebates Work

HOMES rebates are performance-based, rewarding greater energy savings.

Pre-upgrade assessment

Home energy audit to establish baseline consumption.

Qualified contractor work

Improvements completed by registered contractors.

Performance verification

Post-upgrade assessment confirms energy savings achieved.

Important Requirements

Key program requirements and considerations.

Income eligibility

Must meet program income limits based on area median income.

Primary residence

Property must be your primary residence, not rental or vacation home.

Registered contractors

Work must be completed by program-approved contractors.
