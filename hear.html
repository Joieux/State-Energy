HEAR Program Overview

Home Electrification & Appliances

The HEAR program provides rebates for high-efficiency electric appliances and related upgrades including heat pump water heaters, electric cooking equipment, and electrical panel upgrades to support electrification.

Eligible Appliances

HEAR program covers high-efficiency electric appliances and electrification equipment.

Heat pump water heaters

Energy-efficient electric water heating with up to $1,750 rebate.

Electric cooking

Electric ranges, cooktops, and induction cooking equipment.

Heat pump space heating

Efficient electric heating and cooling systems.

Electrical upgrades

Panel upgrades and wiring to support new electric appliances.

Rebate Amounts

Fixed rebate amounts based on appliance type and efficiency ratings.

Heat pump water heater

Electric range

Heat pump HVAC

Rebates vary based on system size and efficiency rating.

Important Requirements

Key program requirements and eligibility criteria.

Income limits

Must meet program income requirements based on household size.

Primary residence only

Appliances must be installed in your primary residence.

Certified appliances

Equipment must meet program efficiency and certification standards.
